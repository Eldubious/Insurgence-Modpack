Version 1.1.3
    Additions:
    - Added mods:
        - Tool Belt
        - Dark Mode Everywhere
        - REBIRTH: Paintings
        - Paintings N' Discs Backport
        - Dark Paintings
        - BetterF3
        - Yucutan Combat
    - Added Resource Pack:
        - Tool Belt Retextured
    - The questbook shop has new inventory added after the player enters the
        dimensional dungeons for the first time
    - Added "mysterious conversion" recipes to JEI to show that some items are converted in-world
    - There are more tips for loading screens

    Changes:
    - The game window will now launch maximized
    - Some recipes changed:
        - The Bibliophage is now crafted in the enchanting apparatus
        - The Bibliophage can also be duplicated with infusion enchanting
        - Various alloys are now made in the basin with a mechanical mixer
        - You can change the color of corundum in the molecular metamorpher
        - Dragonskin can now be made into dragon scales
        - Various cooking recipes have been changed
        - Crystal obelisks can be crafted for those who do not know how to make them normally
    - Some tarot cards' abilities have changed:
        - The World: Slowness IV -> VI
        - The World: range: 3 -> 8
        - Judgement: Double damage chance 4% -> 10%
        - The Tower: Fall damage reduction 25% -> 60%
    - You can no longer pet any entity
    - The traveler's compass now costs a little experience to use
    - All enemies in the dimensional dungeons have been buffed significantly:
        - All enemies have +1 level to their potion effects
        - All enemies have increased base hp
        - Enemies which shoot arrows do 25% more damage and their arrows fly 50% faster
        - Melee enemies have 40% life steal and shred through 50% of protection
        - Melee Illagers deal increased knockback, and have +40% knockback resistance
        - Skeletons carry tipped arrows
    - World gen changes:
        - Corundum will now also spawn in The Depths
        - Modded ores should now also spawn in The Depths
        - You can find pink salt geodes in The Depths
        - Stella Arcanum generates exclusively in the Dragonrealm
        - Aurum Trees and yellow orchids can now generate in the Dragonrealm

    Fixes:
    - The stronghold eye quests in the questbook should now complete properly
    - Enemies in the Dimensional Dungeons can spawn again
    - Necromancers now spawn instead of Pyromancers in the Dimensional Dungeons
    - Backpack enemies will no longer play music discs if they spawn with a jukebox upgrade
        (Hopefully this prevents the pancake disc from playing randomly)
    - Added more effect and enchantment descriptions
    - The vein mining enchantment does not work on raw redstone blocks anymore,
        but it does work on runic ores and deepslate arcane crystal ore now
